### 🚀 About me

I'm a Computer Science student at **Universidade de Fortaleza**, currently interning at the **Laboratory of Technology and Innovation (LTI)** where I build AI agents and intelligent systems. Outside the lab, I'm constantly shipping side projects — I love taking ideas from "what if?" to working products people can actually use.

- 🤖 Focused on **AI agents** that understand goals and execute tasks autonomously
- 🛠️ Bridging polished frontends with smart AI-powered backends
- 🌱 Currently learning **NestJS** and going deeper into backend architecture
- 💡 Always cooking up something new — usually with AI baked in

---

### 🔭 What I'm working on

| Project | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| 📰 **Tech Daily** | A Python bot that scrapes TechCrunch & Hacker News, processes headlines via the Gemini API, and ships a daily newspaper-style digest |
| 📋 **PlanoPro** | React-based freemium web app that turns a few user answers into a fully AI-generated business plan |
| 🎓 **Praça** *(TCC)* | C2C marketplace built around Fortaleza's local identity, featuring AI-powered dynamic pricing and Pix-based escrow |
